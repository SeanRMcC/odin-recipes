Birria Tacos

Description:

This delicious meal, popularized by LA street cuisine, is an amalgamation of mexican flavors, served alongside a consame used to dip the tacos in. Making this for a party, family gathering, or just for your self will be the best decision you will ever make!

Ingredients:

  • Bone in short ribs
  • Chuck roast
  • White onion
  • Quesdilla cheese
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Guajillo peppers
  • Bay leaves
  • Oregano, cumin, salt, chili powder
  • Chicken bouillon
  • Corn tortillas

Steps:

  1. Get a large pot (like, very large)
  2. Prepare the vegetables (cut, peel, and remove seeds)
  3. Add every ingredient (except chili powder) to your large pot and fill it to the top with water. Cover the pot and simmer
  4. Skim the top of the broth, removing debris
  5. Take out the peppers and blend, add a little broth if needed
  6. Continue to simmer for more than 3 hours. Remove lid & stir every 40 minutes
  7. Skim off grease and save to fry the tortillas in
  8. Remove vegetables (just broth and meat now remaining)
  9. Chop up the beef and add it to your fried tortilla with cheese
  10. Serve with broth and enjoy!
