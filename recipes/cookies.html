Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description:

Do I even need a description? Who doesn't like cookies? Make these fan favorites at risk of breaking your grandmother's heart!

Ingredients:

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Get a bowl large enough for the ingredients
  2. Beat in the sugar and butter
  3. Continue by beating the eggs and vanilla, adding them to the bowl
  4. Add the flour and chips to the mix
  5. Mix unitl you have a good dough
  6. Roll out the dough onto a baking sheet
  7. Bake until you have beautiful, golden brown edges
